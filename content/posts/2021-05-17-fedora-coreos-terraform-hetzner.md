Recently i decided to bring up a vm on the Hetzner Cloud in addition to
my local lab environment. I don't want my local lab environment to be always
online for security and power consumming reasons, but i need some services that do. For example i want Wireguard for VPN and Nginx for serving content like ignition files. 

Because i like Fedora, immutable filesystems and i am really diving into k3s, i want to deploy Fedora CoreOS on Hetzner Cloud.
