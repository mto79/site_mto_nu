Semper exploro **("I am always exploring")** is the motto of this personal blog. 
It is also a line which can be found on a Earth Starfleet Command Emblem from the series Star Trek Enterprise. Which, i love to watch!

As a grumpy bearded sysadmin in the day time i'm doing professional exploration for <a href="https://www.nationaalarchief.nl/" target="_blank">The National Archives of the Netherlands</a> and in the evenings my personal exploration. Working in an archive makes you aware, that sometimes you want to register and share some piece of information with others or with yourself (for that matter) sometime at a latter point the future. 

Writing blog articles is not my strongest virtue, so please consider everything i post on this site as **foolish rubbish**! This blog is mainly to have just the oppurtunity to throw some crazy **(sysadmin/programming/techie/peronal)** thoughts on the internet. 

Besides that i just wanted to play with <a href="https://gohugo.io/" target="_blank">Hugo</a> "The world's fastest framework for building websites". So creating my own blog makes a great usecase for that.
